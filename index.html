About

Oishi, a snack brand recognized as a “Shanghai Famous Brand,” has recently teamed up with the Korean girl group TWICE for a new ad campaign called “O Wow! O Wow!”. They officially announced their partnership with the group on September 18 through its social media accounts with videos of the members enjoying the local snacks inspired by “What is Love?” MV. The collaboration between the popular snack brand and the K-pop sensation is a match made in heaven, bringing together the best of both worlds.
